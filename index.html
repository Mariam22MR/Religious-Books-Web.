The importance of religious books

The importance of religious books in daily life A guide for guidance and thinking Religious books are an important source of inspiration and guidance in the lives of many people. These books contain teachings and values ​​that influence individuals in their daily lives and effectively guide them to the path of goodness and spirituality. The role of thesereligious books goes beyond ensuring psychological and spiritual balance and providing a moral framework to guide the behavior of individuals in society.

Religious books are considered one of the treasures of civilization because they convey the meaning of tolerance and encourage building bridges of understanding between different religions. A correct understanding of religious values ​​and teachings plays an important role in promoting tolerance and cultural understanding.

Alrahiq Almakhtum

Research into the Prophet’s biography

Author: Safi ur Rahman Mubarakpuri
Country: Saudi Arabia
the language: Arabic
publisher: Oli Al-Nahy Media Production
Publication date: 1422 AH
the topic: Biography of the Prophet , Histor
number of pages: 512 pages

“Alrahiq Almakhtum” is a study in the biography of the Prophet, may the best blessings and peace be upon its owner, written by His Eminence Sheikh Safi al-Rahman al-Mubarakpuri. It was presented in the Muslim World League’s competition in the noble biography of the Prophet, which was announced at the conference held in Pakistan in the year 1396 AH, and the research won On first place in it.
